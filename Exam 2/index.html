  Order of Assassin were founded by mysterious individual known to Outsiders as the Old Man Of The Mountain or he has known as Hassan i-Sabbah.

  After succesion crisis in the fatimid caliphate, a powerful ismaali shia Muslim dynasty that ruled much of north Africa in the Middle East. The succesion crisis was in Cairo it took place between two of the Caliphate princess, the eldest of the two princess Nazar  was passed over for rule in favor of his younger brother Al - Musta'li.
After a brief power struggle, prince Nazar was executed. And Nazar follower were force to flee east into Persia. Thre they founded a new branch of Shia Islam called Nizari Ismalism, under the guidance of a charismatic missionary by the name of Hassan i-Sabbah.

  But life wasn't easy for the followers of this new doctrine, and at the time persia was occupied by the powerful Seljuk Empire, as Sunni Muslims the seljuk considered nazari beliefs to be heretical a view that was also shared by the country's sheer minority.
And Hassan spent years travelling persia discreetly spreading the word of Nizari Ismalism, and attracting new followers to the course.

  Soon enough Hassan just found the place Alamut Castle which served as the Assassin's headquarters.
